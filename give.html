Are you interesting in helping our cause? We are currently in need of volunteers, partners, and donations.

Volunteer Opportunities

We currently have need of volunteers for the positions listed below. If you are interested, please email us and include a resume. If you feel like you have something to offer that we haven't listed, feel free to contact us. We're happy to hear new ieas.

Board of Directors:   We have vacancies in our Board of Directors. If you are an experienced leader passionate about our cause, we'd love to hear from you! Members of the Board of Directors are responsible for oversight, administration, and leadership of the organization. Attendance is required at quarterly board meetings.

Curriculum Developers:   If you have expertise in a field covered in our training programs and would like to help us develop the curriculum, we'd love your help!

Software Developers:   We are currently working on a software application related to our Lean Prepper project.

Fundraising Volunteers:   We need help with our fundraising campaign. This includes social media posts, website content, grant writing, networking, and building relationships.

Partnerships

We are looking for partnerships with a variety of organizations who may be able to offer services to help with our cause. This can include, but is not limited to, services such as housing, food, immigrant and refugee services, ESL, or scholarships. Please contact us if you are interesting is discussing how we might fill in each others' missing puzzle pieces.

Donations

As a startup non-profit organization, we have need of monetary donations. Although our goal is to become a self-reliant organization able to fully cover our own operational costs in order to allow 100% of donations to go to direct assistance of needy individuals, we currently need help with operational costs as well as assistance towards the needy. We respect the sacrifice of our donors and will use donations to where we deem they will be best put to use.
