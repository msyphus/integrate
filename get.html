Get Service

Whether you are interested in becoming self-reliant or just further developing your current self-reliance, we have a career development training camp for you! We offer programs in the following areas:

Accounting and Finance:   Learn the ins and outs of clerical duties related to accounting and finance. This program will prepare the student for a clerk position. Students will learn basic principles of the field along with commonly used tools.

Agricultural Technology:   Students in this program will learn basic scientific, engineering, and commercial principles behind commercial food production. This program will cover horticulture, terrestrial animal culture, and aquaculture. An emphasis in one of these areas may be chosen. Graduates of this program will be prepared to work for food production facilities as a technician.

Business Administration:   This program covers principles of business administration, entrepreneurship, and leadership. Students will graduate with a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt and be prepared to enter the workforce in office clerk, executive assistant, other similar positions. Interested students may also receive assistance with developing and executing a business plan.

Food Safety:   Principles of food science, food service, and food safety will be covered in this program to prepare students to work in food production or food service operations in positions related to quality control, government regulations, and food safety. Graduates will receive HACCP certification and a food handler's permit (if necessary).

Laboratory Testing:   This program covers quality assurance, quality control, and laboratory testing of manufactured products. Students will learn about sampling methods, laboratory equipment and procedures, measurement system analysis, experimental design, using databases, and audit procedures. Graduates will receive a Lean Six Sigma Black Belt and ISO 17025 training certificate in order to be prepared to work as a laboratory or quality technician.

Operations Management:   Learn how to manage departments, teams, and projects. This program covers department management, leadership, project management, quality management, warehouse, and production principles. Students will graduate with a Lean Six Sigma Black Belt and ISO 9001 training certificate to be prepared to work in a production, warehouse, operational excellence, or quality control position.

Research and Development:   Students in this program will learn how to conduct scientific and market research, design experiments, and design products. Graduates will receive a Lean Six Sigma For Design Black Belt and be prepared to enter the workforce on R&D or product design teams.

Sales and Marketing:   Learn how to sale products and services, conduct market research, run a marketing campaign, improve SEO, and even design graphics. Graduates will be prepared to become sales, marketing, or graphic design professionals.

Software Development   This program teaches principles, tools, and coding languages for developing software applications and websites. Students may choose to lead their own team to develop an app in lieu of an internship. Graduates will be prepared to become developers in an Agile environment.

Free Admission

To quality for free admission to a training program, you must meet all of the following criteria:

  • Must be at least 18 years old
  • Must be unemployed or underemployed
  • Household income must be below the nationally recognized poverty line
  • You lack credentials to get established in a good career

You will get vocational training, an internship, job search coaching, and lifestyle coaching. You will work with a coach to choose a vocational field and develop goals for your program. To remain in the program, you must fulfill an internship and maintain progress towards your goals.

Paid Program

If you do not meet the criteria for free services, you are still welcome to join our training program, but must pay tuition. Additionally, you must meet all of the following criteria:

  • Must be at least 18 years old
  • Must have completed a high school diploma or GED
  • Must be fluent in English

Certain programs also require a basic assessment to verify you have the foundational knowledge required for successful completion.

Products

In addition to our training camps, we also develop software application products. These apps will be made available for download as they are completed.
